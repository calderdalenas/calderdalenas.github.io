The first Calderdale Autism Strategy was produced in 2012. This is the second local strategy covering the next three years. The Autism Strategy Group consists of people with Aspergers and family members and staff from Adult Social Care, the Clinical Commissioning Group, South West Yorkshire Trust, Health, Education, Job Centre Plus and Criminal Justice sector, NAS, voluntary organisations and providers. The group meets quarterly to oversee the implementation of the strategy and action plan.
