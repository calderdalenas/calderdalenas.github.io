Climbing High with Calderdale NAS

Jan 17, 2016 â€¢ Luke Brunning

Our first event of 2016 was at ROKT Climbing Gym in Brighouse. We started off bouldering and caving, with everyone choosing whatever they felt comfortable with doing. There was a lot of stairs to walk up to get to the first activities which got our legs working. I was very impressed by how brave everyone who crawled into the very small hole for the caving was! The bouldering was tricky but everyone soon got the hang of it. Once we were all warmed up we went down to the huge climbing wall which I felt dizzy just looking at. However our fearless climbers, once they were strapped up and securely roped to the instructors, took no time to scale the dizzying heights! All in all it was a great start to the year and really blew away the January cobwebs. It was also good to support a local business that had been affected by the recent floods. Watch out for our next event which is rollerskating in February.
